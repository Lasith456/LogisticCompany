What is TRANS ASIA FREIGHT & LOGISTIC ?

Company Profile

Trans Asia Freight and Logistics (Pvt) Limited was incorporated in year 2005 as a result of phenomenal growth of its parent company Trans Asia clearing and forwarding which was incorporated in year 2000. TAFL has been set up in the most prestigious commercial district of Colombo. While starting with a small staff, substantial growth has been achieved and establish as medium size freight and clearing company in Sri Lanka and overseas with more than 40 Experience Staff.

Trans Asia Clearing and Forwarding is fully owned subsidiary company of Trans Asia Freight and Logistics (Pvt) Limited operations presently holding license from sri Lankan customs,ports authority, central bank of sri lankan and ministry of shipping and transport to carryout clearing and forwarding and freight Operations.
